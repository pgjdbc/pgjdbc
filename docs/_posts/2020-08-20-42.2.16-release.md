## [42.2.16] (2020-08-20)

### Fixed
- Arrays sent in binary format are now sent as 1 based. Fixes [issue 1860](https://github.com/pgjdbc/pgjdbc/issues/1860) in [PR 1863](https://github.com/pgjdbc/pgjdbc/pull/1863).
